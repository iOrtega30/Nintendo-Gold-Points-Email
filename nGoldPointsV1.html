About Gold Points

Not sure what My Nintendo Gold Points are? The short version is
that they are a way to save on digital purchases. You can earn
Gold Points from eligible digital purchases on Nintendo eShop
and Nintendo.com, and then apply them to future eligible digital
purchases.

So basically, the more Gold Points you have now, the more you
can save later!

You can check your current balance in Nintendo eShop on your
device: Just click your user icon in the top-right corner to pop into
your Account information. You can also visit my.nintendo.com for
more info.

How to redeem Gold Points

First, locate the game or item you want on Nintendo eShop or
Nintendo.com and go through the normal purchase steps. Then,
on the purchase confirmation page, select the "Redeem points"
button. Follow the instructions and you'll see your savings.

You can choose how many Gold Points you want to use**and, if
necessary, pay for the remaining balance with your Nintendo
eShop funds or other payment methods.
